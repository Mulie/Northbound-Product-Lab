Who We Are

Northbound Product Lab is a digital product management consultancy with a focus on technology and design strategy. We partner with growth-stage companies to build better products, optimize user experiences, and drive measurable business outcomes.

Our approach combines deep product expertise, technology insight, and design thinking. We help teams navigate complex product decisions, identify growth opportunities, and execute strategies that turn digital products into competitive advantages.
